Spring salad

Description

The other day for lunch I prepared this fresh, simple and nutritious salad that I will never get tired of eating, you absolutely have to try it too!

Ingredients

  • 4 Zucchini
  • 6/8 Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Block of vegetable feta
  • 2 Avocados
  • 600g Chickpeas already cooked
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • q.b Extra virgin olive oil
  • q.b. Salt
  • q.b Pepper
  • q.b Lemon zest
  • q.b Lemon juice
  • 1 Handful pumpkin seeds
  • 1 Handful sunflower seeds

Steps

  1. Cut the courgettes into slices and sauté them in a pan with a little oil, a clove of garlic and a little salt for about 10 minutes.
  2. Cut the courgettes into slices and sauté them in a pan with a little oil, a clove of garlic and a little salt for about 10 minutes.
  3. Also cut the vegetable feta into cubes and set aside.
  4. Finally, remove the pulp from the avocados and season with salt, oil, pepper, lemon zest and juice.
  5. Now assemble your salad starting with the cooked chickpeas and continuing with the cherry tomatoes, the vegetable feta, the courgettes and the avocado. Finish with some pumpkin and sunflower seeds, a sprinkling of pepper and some roughly chopped basil.
  6. Mix well to combine all the ingredients and adjust the salt if necessary.
