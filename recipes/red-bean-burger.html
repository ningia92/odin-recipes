Red bean burger

Description

If you want to taste something delicious and homemade, without giving up the pleasure of a burger, which is typically eaten outside, try this delicious recipe and be ready to change your mind. They are very tasty and also excellent for filling a nice sandwich, perhaps with a little hummus, tomato and caramelized onions, as I did.

Ingredients

  • 280g Cooked red beans
  • 250g Grilled peppers
  • 3/4 Tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • 2 Tablespoons sautéed onion
  • 1 Teaspoon soy sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tablespoon parsley
  • 1/2 Teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven in static mode at 220 °C. Pour the red beans into a blender. Add the grilled and chopped peppers, onion, chopped parsley, paprika and soy sauce. Finish with salt and pepper.
  2. Chop but not completely: as soon as the consistency seems malleable, stop, so that the burgers maintain a slightly grainy consistency. Transfer the mixture just obtained into a bowl and add the breadcrumbs.
  3. As soon as the mixture becomes malleable, start creating the burgers with your hands and place them on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Grease them with a drizzle of oil and cook in a static oven for about 20 minutes, turning them halfway through cooking. In the end you should get burgers that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.
